DOMESTIC VIOLENCE

Domestic violence is made up of a variety of abusive and intimating actions against an intimate current or former partner.

About Domestic Violence

Domestic violence happens when a relationship between a man and a woman which males seeks to assert some power over through acts of violence or abuse to the female partner in a domestic setting. Domestic violence is one of the biggest issue that we face in Australia. Domestic violence includes emotional, physical, sexual, psychological and economical abuse.

The primary target audience applies to women and children in Australia. Statistics shows the latest facts and figures on how women experience physical and emotional abuse by current or former partner since the age of 15. The current statistics from the white ribbon website show that many young people are also affected and exposed to the terror of domestic violence, experiencing child abuse from parents. This leads to the impact on their social well-being and mental health.
